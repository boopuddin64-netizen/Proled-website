Our Story

Engineering Excellence from Eleme, Rivers State

PROLED Engineering Services Limited is a CAC-registered Nigerian private engineering company, duly incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) 2020. Headquartered in Edaramu Estate, Aleto, Eleme LGA, Rivers State, we provide professional, precision-driven engineering solutions to industrial and commercial clients throughout Rivers State.

We were established with a clear mandate: to close the gap between the demand for high-quality engineering services and the availability of certified, professional, and reliable engineering firms serving local industrial and commercial facilities in Rivers State.

From our base in Eleme LGA - one of the most industrially active local government areas in Nigeria - we are uniquely positioned to serve the region's diverse engineering needs. At PROLED, we believe that great engineering is not just about technical skills - it is about accountability, documentation, safety, and delivering on every commitment made to a client.

"These principles guide everything we do, from the first inquiry to final project handover."
